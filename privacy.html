Privacy Policy for MyHabits

Effective date: March 27, 2026

MyHabits ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the MyHabits mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.

1.2 Data Stored Locally

All user data (such as habits, tasks, and progress) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data is not transmitted to us.

1.3 Purchases (Pro Version)

If you purchase the Pro version of the App, payments are processed via third-party services:

  • Google Play Store
  • RevenueCat

RevenueCat may collect information necessary to manage subscriptions, such as purchase status or anonymous identifiers.

We do not have access to your payment details.

More info: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

1.4 Notifications

The App may request permission to send notifications.

  • Notifications are optional
  • You can disable them anytime in system settings
  • No notification data is stored externally

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality
  • Enable notifications
  • Manage subscriptions via RevenueCat

We do not sell or share your data.

3. Data Sharing

We only share data with:

  • Google Play (payments)
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions)

4. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not store data on external servers.

5. Data Retention

You can delete all data by uninstalling the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13.

7. Your Rights

All data is controlled locally on your device.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected by updating the date.
